IT Specialist

Job Description:

responsible for installing, maintaining, and troubleshooting computer hardware and software systems within an organization, providing technical support to users, ensuring network security, and implementing new technological solutions to address business needs, often including tasks like system upgrades, data backup, and user access management



Salary: ~84,113 per year


Job Requirements:

must have a bachelor's degree in computer science, information technology, or a related field

must have experience with computer systems and networks

must have strong problem-solving and communication skills

must be able to work well independently and as part of a team
